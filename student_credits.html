Every semester, the system will conduct evaluations to identify sections eligible for certificates. The certificate will be granted to sections demonstrating cleanliness and tidiness in every classroom or laboratory they are assigned to. Sections less likely to be voted as the dirtiest will qualify for the certificate. The evaluation process will be transparent and based on objective criteria.

The system will identify and announce sections meeting the cleanliness criteria for the certificate. Sections meeting the cleanliness standards will be awarded the certificate, and the students will find their certificate in the library given by Ms. Chesca.
